ZYQENGIMGA Koi Fish Door Curtains

Transform your home with these beautiful Koi Fish Door Curtains that blend style and utility. Designed with a calming cherry blossom pattern, they bring a touch of Japanese-inspired elegance to any doorway. The 52-inch width and 72-inch length ensure a perfect fit for most doors, suitable for use in living rooms, kitchens, bedrooms, and more. Made from light-blocking polyester fabric, they provide 45%-50% privacy and help regulate indoor temperature for added comfort. The grommet design, featuring a 1.6-inch inner diameter, simplifies installation and keeps the curtains secure. These curtains are not only durable but also machine washable for effortless care, making them an excellent choice for anyone seeking long-lasting, stylish solutions for door coverings.
