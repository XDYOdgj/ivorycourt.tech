Lahome Moroccan Trellis Washable Rug

Elevate your home with the Lahome Moroccan Trellis Washable Rug, designed to bring a touch of elegance and comfort to any space. Inspired by the rich aesthetics of Moroccan art, this rug showcases a timeless vintage floral pattern mixed with soft faded tones, making it a perfect fit for both traditional and modern interiors.

Constructed from ultra-soft faux wool fibers, it offers a plush feel underfoot and is ideal for bare feet or gentle steps. The stain-resistant and non-shedding surface ensures your rug stays clean and fresh, even in high-traffic areas. Its low pile design is especially great for families with children or pets, as it minimizes hair and dander buildup and discourages chewing or scratching.

Designed for convenience and durability:

  • Features a trendy faded style with a unique floral pattern
  • Soft faux wool fibers for comfort underfoot
  • Stain-resistant and non-shedding surface
  • Low pile design ideal for pets and kids
  • TPE non-slip backing for secure placement and safety underfoot

This rug is not only stylish but also highly practical, with a thin profile that allows for effortless placement in tight spaces, under furniture, or in entryways without creating tripping hazards. Its machine-washable feature adds to its appeal, making it easy to clean with just a vacuum, broom, or damp cloth.

Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your living room or a functional yet beautiful addition to your bathroom or kitchen, the Lahome Moroccan Trellis Rug is a versatile choice that delivers both style and value. It's the perfect way to create a warm, inviting atmosphere while keeping your floors protected and tidy.
