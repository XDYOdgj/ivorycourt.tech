Elegant Protection for Your AC Line Sets

The Mini Split Wall Sleeve is designed to shield your air conditioning line sets from damage and environmental factors, while also enhancing the visual appeal of your outdoor unit. Its UV-resistant PVC construction ensures durability and resilience against harsh weather conditions, offering long-term reliability and a clean, modern look.

Featuring a built-in high-quality air filter cotton, this sleeve improves indoor air quality by capturing up to 99% of airborne particles as small as 0.01 microns. This means better air circulation, improved comfort, and efficient performance of your air conditioning system without compromising on quality or aesthetics.

Designed for Simplicity and Performance

With a simple one-piece design, this wall sleeve makes installation quick and hassle-free. It fits standard 8.66-inch lengths perfectly, concealing unsightly pipes while ensuring optimal protection. The sleeve is also compatible with most 2.48-inch pipe diameters, making it versatile for a wide range of installations.

Whether you're upgrading your AC system or looking for a reliable outdoor solution, this product is ideal for both residential and commercial applications. It seamlessly blends into your outdoor setup, offering a professional look without the complexity of traditional covers or fittings.
