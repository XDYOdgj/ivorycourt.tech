Efficient Cooling for Your Home

The Gulrear Portable AC Window Vent Kit is a game-changing accessory for anyone looking to enhance their home's cooling system. Designed with convenience and performance in mind, this vent kit offers a perfect solution for your portable AC unit, whether you have a double-hung window or a sliding patio door.

Key Features

  • Universal coupler that fits 5.1" to 5.9" hoses for compatibility with most major AC brands including LG, Hisense, and Midea
  • Built-in double-thread design for a secure and stable connection, eliminating gaps for more efficient cooling and reduced noise
  • Two high-quality weather strippings that ensure a tight seal and better insulation, keeping cool air in and hot air out
  • Easy adjustments with slider nuts to achieve a perfect fit for your window size and type
  • Durable, long-lasting materials that stand up to daily use and maintain a clean, modern appearance

Why Choose This Vent Kit?

Designed to simplify installation and improve cooling performance, this vent kit is ideal for hot summer days or when you need better ventilation. Its 6.5-inch wide square hole gives more space on both sides of your window, making it easier to adjust and fit. The 13-foot weatherproof filling tape ensures comprehensive sealing, preventing air leaks and boosting the effectiveness of your AC unit. High-temperature resistant aluminum tape provides a secure, flexible attachment that allows the vents to move smoothly without tilting or falling.

Perfect for Any Window Type

This versatile vent kit works with a wide range of window styles, including vertical and horizontal sash windows, as well as small sliding windows. Whether you are looking to upgrade your current setup or install a new portable AC unit, the Gulrear Portable AC Window Vent Kit delivers reliable performance and an efficient cooling experience without compromising on aesthetics.
