Effortless Sealing for Your Window Air Conditioner

BJADE's adjustable window AC side panels are the ultimate solution for keeping your home comfortable year-round. With a flexible frame that can be extended or shortened, they offer a custom fit for most window AC units ranging from 5000 to 14000 BTU.

Why Choose BJADE's Side Panels?

  • Adjustable frame size for 5000-14000 BTU window AC units
  • High-quality material for superior insulation and weather sealing
  • Extra wide design fits various window sizes for a snug fit
  • Easy to cut and extend for custom installation
  • Includes two panels, weather strip, hardware, and step-by-step instructions

Whether you're looking to stay cool in the summer or warm in the winter, these panels are an essential accessory for your window AC unit. Stay Comfortable, Save on Energy

By maintaining a secure fit, these panels help retain indoor comfort and cut down on unnecessary AC use. That means less energy consumption, lower bills, and a more sustainable way to keep your home at the perfect temperature. Enjoy peace of mind knowing your window is fully protected from harsh weather conditions.
